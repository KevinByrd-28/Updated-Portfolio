About Me

Hello! My name's Kevin and thanks for visiting my portfolio!
I've operated missions on behalf of my government in nearly every continent on the Earth while I served as an Anti-Terrorism Officer in the US Air Force Reserves and a Private Military Contractor (PMC). I even spent some time as a Sheriff's Deputy, patroling one of the highest violent crime jurisdictions in the state of South Carolina. Then fast forward to December 2019 and the COVID Pandemic of 2020 - when I decided to change my life forever and enrolled in Eleven Fifty Academy's Software Development Bootcamp!
Since then, I have had the privilege of working for a couple of different firms in a Full Stack role and have been freelancing single page applications and websites for local small businesses in my community. I am currently with FLEXcon Company, Inc. as their sole Web Developer, working on rebuilding their online platform, customer portal and ecommerce system.
When I'm not focused on work, I enjoy going out on adventures with my wife and two kids! When no family adventures are in sight, I enjoy taking part in amatuer motorsports events and building project cars. I am currently in the middle of building an adventure rig out of a 1995 Range Rover Classic!
